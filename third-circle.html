Third Circle: Gluttony

Enduring the Filthy Slush

As you descend into the Third Circle, you will feel the incessant icy rain falling upon your back, and the ground will be filled with more and more filthy mud the farther in you get. Here, the gluttonous wallow in the vile slush and are tortured and torn apart by Cerberus, the guard of this circle of Hell. The Third Circle is one of the most difficult regions of Hell to travel through because of how difficult the environment is and the presence of Cerberus. However, getting through here is worth it to see the more interesting and exciting lower circles of the Inferno.

Trudging Through the Circle

You will find that it is incredibly difficult to travel through the slush due to how disgusting and sticky it is. Unfortunately, there are no transit options here other than simply walking through it. Make sure to bring an umbrella to guard against the rain and some thick boots that will allow you to travel through the slush without getting bogged down.

Tip: Getting Past Cerberus

The three-headed beast Cerberus guards this circle, and he can be very difficult to get past if you accidentally catch his attention. However, you can pass through him if you take some of the mud on the ground and fill his mouths with it. Be careful not to get your hand bitten off in the process, though!
