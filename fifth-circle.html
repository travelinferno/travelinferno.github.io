Fifth Circle: Wrath

Battles on the River Styx

In the Fifth Circle, the wrathful lay on the River Styx. You can watch the damned battle each other on the surface of the Styx as you travel across by ferry. Phlegyas can ferry you across the Styx; while he may be reluctant, he should be able to let you across as long as you ask confidently. After crossing the Styx, you will encounter the gates to the City of Dis. Be cautious here, as you may encounter the Furies or Medusa. Make sure not to look at Medusa or else you will be turned to stone! Always remember that you have the right to travel through Hell, and reminding any hostile figures of that should neutralize their aggression.

Tip: Beneath the River's Surface

While you can clearly see the actively wrathful fighting each other on the surface of the Styx, you may not catch the passively wrathful who are submerged in the marshy water. You can observe their lost hope as they eternally sulk over themselves while you are ferried across the river.

Entering Dis

Firstly, if you make it this far down the Inferno, congratulations! It is not easy to get this far as a traveler. Now listen carefully. The walls of Dis are guarded by fallen angels and it is nearly certain that they will not allow living travelers like you in. You must search for an angel to let you into the city. There are barely any who linger around the Fifth Circle, so be prepared to scour around. You can tell the difference between the true angel and the fallen angels based on their wings; the true angel has feathered wings, while the fallen angels have bat wings. This is the best currently known way to enter the city; we acknowledge it is difficult, and we wish you luck. This will be one of the most difficult parts of your journey.
