Fourth Circle: Greed

Continuous Heavy Battles

Here, the greedy battle each other using heavy weights they push with their chests as weapons. You must be very cautious when crossing through this circle. Very few of the damned here will be interested in talking, and most of them will be constantly engaged in battle. With heavy weights all around, it's easy to get caught in the battles and be severely injured. When traveling here, move quickly and lightly and try not to attract any attention to yourself.

Tip: Beware of Plutus

Watch out for Plutus, the ancient deity of wealth; he has been reported to chant demonic invocations to passersby. If you encounter him, simply yell at him to be silent and dart away. He should stay where he is and stop bothering you.

Clergymen in Battle

You may notice that many of the damned here were members of the clergy in life. The truth is many of these kinds of men are not truly agents of God, but rather incredibly greedy individuals who seeked to only amass their own wealth in life. Remember this when considering which religious leaders to listen to and follow once you return to the living world.
