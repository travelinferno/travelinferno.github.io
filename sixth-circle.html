Sixth Circle: Heresy

Entombed in Constant Inferno

In the Sixth Circle, heretics are trapped in tombs that are constantly on fire. Heretics are people who deny the truth of religious doctrine, such as the Epicureans, who claim that the soul dies with the body. You can also encounter Farinata degli Uberti, the famous Ghibelline (supporter of the Holy Roman Emperor) leader. We strongly recommend that you try to seek out and speak to Farinata on your voyage, as he can give interesting information about life as a soul in Hell and how they perceive the living world.

Seeing the Future

When our founder Dante spoke to Farinata in 1300, Farinata explained that the souls of Hell can't see the present in the living world, but they can see the future. You can ask Farinata or the other souls in Hell about the future during your journey, but be careful what you ask them. Consider whether you really want to know certain things about the future! Additionally, the window the damned have to the future will eventually be shut, which may frustrate them if pried.

Tip: Food? Shelter?

By the time you get to the Sixth Circle, you may realize that you have not yet grown hungry or tired. This is a property of Hell present even for the living who travel here. While you're here, you won't have to worry about food and shelter. So get your mind off of that and continue traveling; focus on the people you speak with and the lessons you are learning!
