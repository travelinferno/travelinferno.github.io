Our Founder

On Maundy Thursday in the year 1300, our founder Dante Alighieri found himself wandering lost in a dark forest. He had been exiled from his home in Florence and did not know what to do with his life. In that forest, he was cornered by three devilish beasts, not sure if he would survive. However, the Lord blessed him on that day. His departed beloved Beatrice sent down his hero and master Virgil to guide Dante through the Inferno. Dante visited every region of that wretched land, and he was able to find divine salvation in Paradise as a result.

For to reach heaven, you must first go through hell.™

That is the founding principle of Travel Inferno, and the principle Dante carried on when he wrote the Divine Comedy, the work that sparked the creation of our organization, providing travel information about Hell since 1321. We implore you, as a follower of Christ and a seeker of salvation, to tread in Dante's footsteps and discover the Inferno for yourself.
